Niranjan Kumar

Georgia Institute of Technology. niranjankumar@gatech.edu

I’m a PhD candidate at Georgia Institute of Technology working with Prof. Irfan Essa and Prof. Sehoon Ha. Before starting my PhD, I was a Master’s student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Gatech and worked at the OLIVES lab with Prof. Ghassan AlRegib.

The goal of my research is to teach embodied agents exploration strategies that improve their understanding of objects around them. See my Projects/Publications/CV for more information.
